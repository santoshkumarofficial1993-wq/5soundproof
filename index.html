From simple DIY fixes to professional acoustic treatments — explore the most effective ways to soundproof your home.

🪟

Soundproof Curtains

Heavy, multi-layered drapes with mass-loaded vinyl cores that block outside noise and reduce echo in any room.

Door Seals & Sweeps

Weatherstripping and automatic door sweeps that eliminate air gaps — one of the most cost-effective soundproofing upgrades.

Doors

🪟

Window Inserts

Acrylic or glass inserts that fit inside existing window frames to create an extra air gap and dramatically reduce noise.

Windows

Acoustic Caulk

Specialized flexible sealant that fills cracks and gaps around windows, doors, and baseboards to block sound leakage.

Sealing

Dense, flexible vinyl sheets that add mass to walls, floors, and ceilings — highly effective at blocking airborne noise.

Walls

Soundproof Drywall

Specially engineered drywall with dense cores and damping layers that outperforms standard drywall by up to 50%.

Walls

Acoustic Foam

Open-cell foam panels that absorb mid-to-high frequencies and reduce flutter echoes in studios and listening rooms.

Treatment

Carpet & Underlay

Floors

Walls
